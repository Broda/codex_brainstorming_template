# Glossary

Normalized vocabulary for governance and planning artifacts.

- Idea: A candidate project concept tracked through lifecycle states.
- Decision: A recorded choice with rationale and consequences.
- ADR: Architecture Decision Record for durable strategic/architectural choices.
- Gate: Review outcome that determines readiness (`pass`, `conditional-pass`, `fail`).
- Conditional-pass: Gate outcome allowing progress with explicit owner-assigned conditions.
- Finalize: Lifecycle action that marks an idea as exported and complete in this repository.
- Export: Immutable `PROJECT_PLAN_PACKET` handoff artifact for downstream build repositories.
